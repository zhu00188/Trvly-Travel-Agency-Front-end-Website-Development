What types of trip styles do you offer?

We offer a variety of trip styles to suit different preferences: Adventure tours for thrill-seekers, Cultural immersion experiences, Relaxation and wellness retreats, Family-friendly vacations, Luxury escapes, and Budget-friendly backpacking trips. Each style is carefully designed to provide authentic experiences while matching your travel preferences and activity level.

How physically demanding are your tours?

Our tours are rated from 1 (easy) to 5 (challenging). Easy trips involve minimal walking and are suitable for all fitness levels. Moderate trips may include 3-5 hours of activity daily. Challenging trips involve strenuous activities like hiking, trekking, or high-altitude adventures. Check each tour's activity level rating before booking to ensure it matches your fitness capabilities.

What is the typical group size?

Our small group tours typically range from 8 to 16 travelers, ensuring an intimate experience where you can connect with fellow adventurers while receiving personalized attention from your guide. This size strikes the perfect balance between social interaction and flexibility, allowing us to access unique locations that larger groups cannot.

Can I book a private group tour?

Absolutely! We offer private group options for families, friends, or corporate teams. Private tours provide ultimate flexibility with customizable itineraries, departure dates, and pace. You'll have a dedicated guide and can tailor activities to your group's interests. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your group size and desired destinations.

What is the minimum age for children on tours?

Age requirements vary by tour type. Our family-friendly tours welcome children aged 5 and up, with special activities designed to engage young travelers. Adventure tours typically require participants to be 12+, while some challenging expeditions have a minimum age of 16. We offer family discounts and can arrange child-friendly accommodations with connecting rooms.

Are your tours suitable for families with young children?

Yes! We have dedicated family tours with age-appropriate activities, shorter travel days, and kid-friendly accommodations. Our guides are experienced in traveling with families and can adjust the pace as needed. We provide recommendations for child-safe destinations, activities that engage different age groups, and can arrange babysitting services at select locations.

Is there a single supplement fee?

Solo travelers are typically required to pay a single supplement for private room accommodations. However, we offer a "room-sharing" option where solo travelers can be matched with another same-gender traveler to avoid the supplement. We also have select tours with no single supplement on specific departure dates, clearly marked on our website.

Will I feel comfortable traveling solo on a group tour?

Absolutely! Many of our travelers journey solo and find our small group tours the perfect way to explore safely while making new friends. Our tour leaders are skilled at facilitating group dynamics and ensuring everyone feels included. Solo travelers appreciate the built-in social aspect while maintaining independence during free time. You'll never feel alone on the adventure!

Can I join a trip if I have a disability?

We welcome travelers with disabilities and strive to make our tours accessible. However, accessibility varies by destination and tour type. Please contact us before booking to discuss your specific needs. We'll provide honest information about physical demands, accommodation accessibility, and available support services. In many cases, we can make arrangements or suggest suitable alternative tours.

What accessibility accommodations do you provide?

We work with accessible hotels, arrange transportation with appropriate vehicles, and can modify itineraries when possible. Our guides are trained in inclusivity and will assist as needed. We recommend booking private tours for maximum flexibility. Please inform us of mobility aids, medical requirements, or special assistance needs at booking so we can make appropriate arrangements.

How do I book a tour?

Booking is easy! Browse our tours online, select your preferred departure date, and click "Book Now." You'll need to fill out a booking form with traveler details and pay a deposit (typically 20-30% of total cost). Full payment is usually due 60 days before departure. You can also call our booking team for personalized assistance or to discuss custom itineraries.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For group bookings over $10,000, we can arrange installment payment plans. All transactions are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. Payment receipts and invoices are automatically emailed after each transaction for your records.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 90+ days before departure receive a full refund minus a $200 administrative fee. 60-89 days: 50% refund. 30-59 days: 25% refund. Less than 30 days: no refund. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment. Cancellations must be submitted in writing via email. Refunds are processed within 14 business days to the original payment method.

Can I change my tour dates after booking?

Yes! Date changes are subject to availability and must be requested at least 60 days before departure. One free date change is allowed per booking; subsequent changes incur a $150 modification fee. If the new tour has a higher price, you'll pay the difference. If it's lower priced, the difference is non-refundable but can be applied as credit toward future bookings within one year.
